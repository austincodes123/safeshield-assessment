CONFIDENTIALITY & NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT

This Confidentiality and Non-Disclosure Agreement (the "Agreement") is entered into by and between Safeshield Insurance LLC, 2777 Summer St, Stamford, CT, United States ("Safeshield"), and Marshelene Beukes, located in South Africa ("Recipient").

1. Purpose

Safeshield intends to disclose certain confidential and proprietary information to Recipient in connection with Recipient's engagement as a Virtual Insurance Assistant. This Agreement governs the handling, protection, and permissible use of such information during and after the term of engagement.

2. Definition of Confidential Information

"Confidential Information" means any and all non-public information disclosed by Safeshield, including but not limited to:

  • Client lists, contact information, policy details, renewal dates, and account data
  • Proprietary technology systems including CASEY, NowCerts configurations, workflows, and scripts
  • Business strategies, financial data, commission structures, pricing models, and operational methods
  • Carrier relationships, appointment details, and underwriting guidelines
  • Training materials, playbooks, internal documentation, and standard operating procedures
  • Marketing strategies, advertising spend, lead sources, and campaign data
  • Employee and contractor compensation, roles, and internal communications
  • Any information marked or reasonably understood to be confidential

3. Obligations of Recipient

Recipient agrees to:

  • Hold all Confidential Information in strict confidence
  • Not disclose, share, publish, post, or distribute any Confidential Information to any third party, including family members, friends, other employers, or online platforms
  • Not use any Confidential Information for any purpose other than performing duties for Safeshield
  • Not photograph, screenshot, copy, download to personal devices, email to personal accounts, or reproduce any Confidential Information by any means
  • Not store any Confidential Information on any device, account, or platform other than those provided and authorized by Safeshield
  • Immediately notify Safeshield of any unauthorized disclosure, access, or use of Confidential Information

4. Non-Solicitation and Non-Circumvention

During the term of engagement and for twenty-four (24) months following its conclusion, Recipient shall not directly or indirectly:

  • Solicit, contact, or service any Safeshield client, lead, prospect, or referral partner for insurance or related services
  • Solicit, recruit, or hire any Safeshield employee, contractor, or team member
  • Circumvent Safeshield to conduct or facilitate insurance business with any of the Company's clients, carriers, or business relationships
  • Use any Confidential Information to compete with Safeshield or to benefit any competing business

5. Intellectual Property and Work Product

All work product created by Recipient in connection with the engagement, including notes, CRM entries, client communications, templates, scripts, workflows, spreadsheets, and data compilations, shall be deemed work made for hire and the exclusive property of Safeshield Insurance LLC.

6. Return of Materials

Upon termination for any reason, Recipient shall immediately return or surrender the company-provided laptop and all Safeshield property, delete all Confidential Information from any device or platform, and confirm in writing that no data has been retained.

7. Liquidated Damages

In the event of any material breach of Sections 3, 4, or 5 of this Agreement, Recipient shall pay to Safeshield liquidated damages in the amount of $12,000 USD (equivalent to six months of Recipient's compensation), in addition to any other remedies available. The Parties agree this represents a genuine pre-estimate of loss and is not intended as a penalty.

8. Remedies and Injunctive Relief

Recipient acknowledges that breach may cause irreparable harm. Safeshield shall be entitled to seek injunctive relief without proving actual damages or posting a bond. Recipient consents to the granting of injunctive relief in any court of competent jurisdiction, including courts in the State of Connecticut and courts in the Republic of South Africa.

9. Acknowledgment of Reasonableness

Recipient acknowledges and agrees that:

  • Recipient will have access to Safeshield's most sensitive confidential information, client relationships, and proprietary systems
  • The restrictions in this Agreement are reasonable and necessary to protect Safeshield's legitimate business interests
  • The restrictions do not impose an undue hardship and do not prevent Recipient from earning a livelihood, provided Recipient does not use Safeshield's Confidential Information or solicit Safeshield's clients
  • Recipient has had the opportunity to review this Agreement, seek independent legal counsel, and negotiate its terms before signing
  • Recipient is entering into this Agreement voluntarily and with full understanding of its implications

10. Governing Law and Jurisdiction

This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Connecticut, United States. To the extent enforcement is sought in South Africa, the provisions shall be interpreted under South African law as required. Safeshield may bring action in either the courts of Connecticut (or binding arbitration in Stamford, CT) or the courts of the Republic of South Africa, at its sole discretion. Recipient consents to both jurisdictions. The prevailing Party shall recover reasonable legal fees and costs.

11. Term and Survival

Confidentiality obligations survive for five (5) years from disclosure or five (5) years following termination, whichever is longer. Non-solicitation obligations survive for twenty-four (24) months following termination.

12. Severability

If any provision is found invalid in any jurisdiction, the remaining provisions remain in full force. Invalid provisions shall be modified to the minimum extent necessary. Invalidity in one jurisdiction does not affect validity in another.

INDEPENDENT CONTRACTOR AGREEMENT

This Independent Contractor Agreement is entered into by and between Safeshield Insurance LLC, 2777 Summer St, Stamford, CT ("Safeshield"), and Marshelene Beukes, located in South Africa ("Contractor").

1. Scope of Services

Contractor shall provide virtual insurance assistant services to Safeshield, including client communication, policy servicing, renewal management, data entry, administrative support, and other duties as assigned. Safeshield may modify the scope at its discretion.

2. Training & Performance

Contractor agrees to participate in all required training, including onboarding (May 1-29, 2026), and ongoing performance reviews. Continued eligibility for compensation is contingent upon satisfactory participation, effort, and compliance with Company expectations.

3. Compensation

Contractor shall receive $2,000 USD per month, paid biweekly ($1,000 per pay period) via Wise to Contractor's South African bank account. Compensation is reviewed annually at year-end. Contractor is solely responsible for all tax obligations in the Republic of South Africa.

4. Work Hours & Availability

Contractor shall be available Monday through Friday, 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM US Eastern Time. Contractor must maintain a reliable high-speed internet connection and a quiet, private workspace suitable for client-facing calls.

5. Equipment

Safeshield will provide a company-managed laptop that remains the sole property of Safeshield at all times. No personal software, applications, accounts, or files may be installed or stored on the device. Safeshield retains the right to monitor, manage, update, and remotely access or wipe the device at any time. The device must be returned or made available for remote wipe within 48 hours upon termination.

6. Independent Contractor Status

Contractor is an independent contractor and not an employee of Safeshield. Contractor is not entitled to benefits, tax withholding, or employment protections. Nothing in this Agreement creates a partnership, joint venture, or employment relationship. Contractor is responsible for completing IRS Form W-8BEN prior to the start date.

7. Termination

Either party may terminate with 14 calendar days written notice via email. Safeshield may terminate immediately for gross misconduct, breach of confidentiality, unauthorized data access, failure to return equipment, abandonment of duties (3+ consecutive days without communication), or misrepresentation of hours worked. Upon termination, all system access is revoked immediately and final payment is processed for work completed through termination date.

8. Ownership of Work Product

All work product, client records, communications, and data created during the engagement are the exclusive property of Safeshield.

9. Confidentiality

Contractor shall comply with the Confidentiality and Non-Disclosure Agreement executed concurrently with this Agreement. All confidentiality obligations survive termination.

10. Indemnification

Contractor shall indemnify and hold harmless Safeshield from any claims, damages, losses, or expenses arising from Contractor's acts, omissions, or breach of this Agreement.

11. Governing Law

Governed by the laws of the State of Connecticut. Disputes resolved by binding arbitration in Stamford, CT, or in the courts of the Republic of South Africa, at Safeshield's discretion.

12. Entire Agreement

This Agreement, together with the Offer Letter, NDA, and Acceptable Use Policy, constitutes the entire agreement. Amendments must be in writing and signed by both Parties.

ACCEPTABLE USE POLICY

This Acceptable Use Policy governs the use of the company-provided laptop and all Safeshield systems, platforms, and accounts by Marshelene Beukes ("User").

1. Company-Provided Device

The laptop provided by Safeshield Insurance LLC is company property and is provided solely for the purpose of performing Safeshield work. User agrees to:

  • Use the device exclusively for Safeshield business and approved purposes
  • Not install any personal software, applications, browser extensions, or VPN services without written approval
  • Not store any personal files, photos, music, or non-work-related content on the device
  • Not allow any other person to use the device for any reason
  • Keep the device physically secure and not leave it unattended in public or shared spaces
  • Report any damage, loss, theft, or malfunction to management immediately

2. System Access & Credentials

User agrees to:

  • Use strong, unique passwords for all Safeshield accounts and change them when directed
  • Never share login credentials with any other person, including family members or colleagues
  • Enable two-factor authentication on all accounts where available
  • Log out of all systems at the end of each workday
  • Report any suspected unauthorized access or security breach immediately

3. Data Handling

User agrees to:

  • Access client data and company information only through authorized Safeshield systems (NowCerts, CASEY, Google Workspace)
  • Never transfer, copy, download, email, or export any client data or company information to personal devices, personal email accounts, USB drives, personal cloud storage, or any unauthorized platform
  • Never take screenshots or photographs of client data, internal dashboards, or proprietary systems
  • Process all client communications through official Safeshield channels only
  • Follow all data handling procedures as outlined in training

User agrees to:

  • Not use the company device for personal social media, streaming, gaming, or non-work browsing during work hours
  • Not access any illegal, inappropriate, or harmful websites or content on the device at any time
  • Not use the device to communicate with clients or third parties through any channel other than official Safeshield platforms
  • Not post any Safeshield-related content, client information, or internal details on any social media platform or public forum

5. Monitoring & Compliance

User acknowledges and agrees that:

  • Safeshield reserves the right to monitor, audit, and log all activity on the company-provided device and Safeshield systems at any time without prior notice
  • Safeshield may remotely access, update, or wipe the device at any time
  • User has no expectation of privacy regarding any activity on the company device or Safeshield systems
  • Safeshield may conduct periodic audits of system access, data handling, and device usage

6. Return of Equipment

Upon termination of the engagement for any reason, User must return the company-provided device or make it available for remote wipe within 48 hours. Failure to comply will result in forfeiture of any outstanding compensation and may result in further legal action.

7. Violations

Any violation of this Acceptable Use Policy may result in immediate termination of the engagement, revocation of all system access, and pursuit of legal remedies including those outlined in the Confidentiality and Non-Disclosure Agreement.
